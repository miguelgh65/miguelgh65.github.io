Demand Predictor for Customer Success

We are developing an artificial intelligence model based on time series prediction. This tool focuses on forecasting the demand for cases depending on the day of the week, month, and even the season of the year. Accurate demand prediction allows for resource optimization, improved customer experience, and anticipation of potential challenges.

To achieve this prediction, we are using advanced technologies and methods in the field of artificial intelligence. Specifically, we have focused on the use of Transformers, an innovative architecture that has proven effective in multiple AI applications, including time series.

The model has been implemented in Python, using the PyTorch library for model construction and training. Additionally, we use Matplotlib for data and time series visualization, allowing for better interpretation and analysis of the results.

An essential feature of our system is the integration with Salesforce through its API. This integration allows us to access case-related data in real-time, and therefore, enhance the accuracy of our predictions by constantly feeding our model with updated data.

In summary, this demand predictor presents itself as a valuable tool for the Customer Success team, providing precise and anticipated insights for better planning and decision-making.
