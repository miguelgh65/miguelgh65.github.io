ST Engineering Idirect via Sortis team

For about 2 years now, I have been working for the client ST Engineering Idirect as an Associate Engineer, focusing on Layer 2 to Layer 3 technical support, as well as the installation and maintenance of satellite networks. This experience has given me the opportunity to familiarize and specialize in a wide range of technologies, tools, and platforms, including Python, networking, Jira, Salesforce, and specialized knowledge in RF.

My technical expertise covers areas such as networking with advanced configurations of Switches, Routers, Firewalls, VLANs, and DMZs; Linux system administration; scripting and automation using Python; and cloud infrastructure management. Additionally, I've used tools like PuTTY, Wireshark, TCPdump, Grafana, Any Desk, and TeamViewer in my daily work. Through reviewing metrics and KPIs, I have identified anomalies and proactively managed potential incidents, always maintaining SLAs with our clients.

An essential part of my role has been the intensive use of Salesforce, where I've not only analyzed and generated performance charts, but also employed AI to predict trends and patterns in the cases. In fact, I've undertaken significant projects in this area, as you can see in my related project.

My interest in data science, combined with my strong technical foundation, has been a potent combination that has allowed me to make significant contributions to projects and the team. With the adoption of agile methodologies, we've ensured the delivery of efficient solutions. I've personally led installations both within the European Union and internationally, being the primary liaison with clients and ensuring quality in each implementation.
