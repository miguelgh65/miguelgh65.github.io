Real Estate Price Predictor

Currently, we are working on an artificial intelligence model designed to predict real estate prices using real data. By leveraging the Idealista API, we have gathered a comprehensive set of information related to house prices, property listings, locations, and other pertinent features.

After thorough analysis, we observed correlations between variables like size, location, and price of the properties. Having identified these correlations, we proceeded to run regressions to estimate house prices. These regressions are executed through traditional methods and deep learning techniques.

Beyond price prediction, we have also developed an image-based classifier that determines the type of dwelling. For instance, from photographs of a property, our model can discern whether it's an apartment, a duplex, a chalet, among others.

In summary, our tool not only estimates a property's value but also categorizes its type based on its images. These advancements provide significant leverage for real estate professionals, offering them precise tools to enhance their decision-making process and deliver a better experience to their clients.
